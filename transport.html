Full Stack Web Development Project: Campus Transportation Website

Built and launched an automated portal for reserving seats on university bus transit services to hometowns.

Project Overview:

  • Designed end-to-end solution spanning intuitive frontend, SQL database backend, and reservation/notification logic.
  • Crafted user-friendly interfaces with HTML/CSS/JS. Developed PHP scripts interacting MYSQL for data integrity.
  • Encrypted and secured storage of passenger personal information and travel details.
